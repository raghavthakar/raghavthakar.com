Hi! I'm Raghav Thakar

I'm a Robotics PhD student at the Autonomous Agents and Distributed Intelligence Lab, in the Collaborative Robotics and Intelligent Systems Institute at Oregon State University. I am advised by Dr Kagan Tumer.

I work on multiagent learning, i.e., learning-based techniques that enable multiple autonomous agents to coordinate and perform complex tasks together. In particular, I am interested in multiagent learning for multi-objective problems, wherein multiagent systems must learn to optimise for and/or balance multiple objectives at once.

If you would like to talk about research, new ideas, or anything else, please do not hesitate in reaching out: thakarr@oregonstate.edu
