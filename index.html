Hi! I'm Raghav Thakar

I'm a Robotics PhD student at the Autonomous Agents and Decision Intelligence (AADI) Lab, part of the Collaborative Robotics and Intelligent Systems (CoRIS) Institute at Oregon State University. I am advised by Dr Kagan Tumer.

I work on enabling autonomous agents to learn good team-oriented behaviour to perform complex cooperative tasks, especially when these tasks involve multiple, simultaneous objectives. Broadly, my research lies at the intersection of multiagent learning, multi-objective optimisation, and robotics. Specifically, I am currently studying multi-objective credit assignment for multiagent systems.
