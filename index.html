Hi! I'm Raghav Thakar

I'm a Robotics PhD student at the Autonomous Agents and Distributed Intelligence (AADI) Lab, part of the Collaborative Robotics and Intelligent Systems (CoRIS) Institute at Oregon State University. I am advised by Dr Kagan Tumer.

I work on enabling autonomous agents to learn good team-oriented behaviour to perform complex cooperative tasks, especially when these tasks involve multiple, simultaneous objectives. Broadly, my research lies at the intersection of multiagent learning, multi-objective learning, and robotics.

If you would like to talk about research, new ideas, or anything else, please do not hesitate in emailing me.
